U20: Video ROM (2732)
U24: ROM AB (2764)
U25: ROM CD #1 (2764)
     ROM CD #2 (2764)
U26: ROM EF #1 (2764)
     ROM EF #2 (2764)

U25 & U26 ROMs are piggybacked with their enable pins switched.
The top ROMs are #1 and the bottom ROMs are #2 -  either both #1 ROMs are
active or both #2 ROMs are active (enable pulled low).
